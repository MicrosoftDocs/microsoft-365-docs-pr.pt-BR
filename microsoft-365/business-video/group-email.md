1. <span data-ttu-id="a8029-107">No Outlook, na guia **Página** Home, escolha **Novo Grupo.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a8029-107">In Outlook, on the  **Home** tab, choose  **New Group**.</span></span>
